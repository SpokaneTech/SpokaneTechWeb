Develop with us

SpokaneTech.org is not just a website, it's a community driven initiative to foster development, knowledge sharing, and collaboration. Whether you are a seasoned developer, new to the industry, of just a hobbyist, your involvement is welcome.

Contribute

We are always looking for contributors to help improve the site. If you have experience with Python, Django, front-end development, databases, devops, (or would like to learn) we would be thrilled to have you on board. You can find our project on GitHub here:
https://github.com/SpokaneTech/SpokaneTechWeb

Follow Development

We strive to document each phase of development through detailed blog posts, giving you insight into the site's progress and the reasoning behind key decisions.
Follow along on: GitHub, Dev.to, or Medium.
